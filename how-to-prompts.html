How-To Article Prompts for Dental Content

1. Introduction Prompt
Write a 2-3 paragraph introduction for a dental blog post on "[TOPIC]". The introduction should:
  • Address practicing dentists and implantologists
  • Provide clinical significance of [TOPIC]
  • Include relevant statistics or clinical cases
  • Present key considerations
  • Write from the perspective of an experienced specialist with over 15 years of clinical practice
Example: "How to Choose the Right Dental Implant Diameter", "How to Manage Peri-implantitis"
