How-To Article Prompts for Dental Content

1. Introduction Prompt
Write a 2-3 paragraph introduction for a dental blog post on "[TOPIC]". The introduction should:
  • Address practicing dentists and implantologists
  • Provide clinical significance of [TOPIC]
  • Include relevant statistics or clinical cases
  • Present key considerations
  • Write from the perspective of an experienced specialist with over 15 years of clinical practice
Example: "How to Choose the Right Dental Implant Diameter", "How to Manage Peri-implantitis"
2. Technical Definition Prompt
As an experienced dental specialist, explain:
  • What is [TOPIC] in clinical practice?
  • Why is [TOPIC] crucial for treatment success?
  • What are the biological and clinical considerations?
  • What are the key limitations and contraindications?
Include scientific references and clinical evidence to support your explanations.
3. Expert Opinion Prompt
You're a dental specialist with expertise in [SPECIFIC AREA]. Write your professional opinion on [TOPIC]. Include:
  • Your clinical experience with [TOPIC]
  • Common mistakes to avoid
  • Critical decision-making factors
  • Real case examples from your practice
  • Evidence-based recommendations
Use professional medical language while maintaining clarity.
4. Comparison Table Prompt
Create a detailed comparison table for [TOPIC] including:
  • Clinical scenarios
  • Technical specifications
  • Advantages and limitations
  • Success rates
  • Risk factors
  • Cost considerations
Base information on clinical studies and current guidelines.
5. Equipment List Prompt
Create a comprehensive list of required equipment and materials for [TOPIC]:
  • Essential equipment
  • Optional but recommended tools
  • Diagnostic instruments
  • Materials and supplies
  • Safety equipment
Include specifications and critical features for each item.
6. Step-by-Step Protocol Prompt
Write a detailed step-by-step protocol for [TOPIC]:
  • Pre-procedure assessment
  • Preparation steps
  • Main procedure steps
  • Post-procedure care
  • Follow-up protocol
Include clinical tips, warnings, and critical checkpoints at each step.
7. FAQ Section Prompt
Generate a comprehensive FAQ section addressing common concerns about [TOPIC]:
  • Questions from both novice and experienced practitioners
  • Complications and their prevention
  • Technical considerations
  • Maintenance and follow-up
  • Patient education aspects
Base answers on clinical evidence and current guidelines.
8. Title Generation Prompt
Write 5 clinically relevant, SEO-optimized titles for the article about [TOPIC]. Titles should:
  • Target dental professionals
  • Include specific clinical scenarios
  • Address common challenges
  • Highlight professional benefits
  • Use relevant keywords

Important Notes:

  • Replace [TOPIC] with your specific dental procedure or technique
  • Adjust technical complexity based on your target audience
  • Include relevant clinical images and diagrams where possible
  • Always verify clinical information with current research
  • Maintain professional tone throughout the article
